Meet Dr. Richards

Dr. Brian Richards was born and raised in Moline, IL and graduated from Moline High School in 1998. He attended The University of Iowa, majoring in Biology and Psychology, before going to dental school at The University of Iowa College of Dentistry. Following his formal dental education, Dr. Richards completed a postgraduate training program, specializing in pediatric dentistry.

Dr. Richards and his dental team have completed over 500 hours of hands-on continuing education at the Las Vegas Institute for Advanced Dental Studies (LVI). LVI is a postgraduate training program emphasizing comprehensive esthetic (cosmetic) dentistry.

Dr. Richards maintains two private dental practices. The Quad City Smile Rejuvenation Center is an adult dental practice that caters to individuals that want the best and most up-to-date dentistry available, with an emphasis on cosmetic dentistry. Two Rivers Pediatric Dentistry primarily emphasizes dental treatment for infants, children and young adults.

Dr. Richards is married to Dr. Anne Bronwen Richards, an orthodontist in Rock Island, IL. They have two children, Brandon and Makena.
